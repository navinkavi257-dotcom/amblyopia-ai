What is Amblyopia?

Amblyopia, commonly called "Lazy Eye", is a vision disorder that develops during childhood. It occurs when one eye does not develop normal vision because the brain favors the other eye. Early diagnosis and treatment are important to prevent permanent vision loss.

Causes

  • Strabismus (crossed eyes)
  • Refractive errors
  • Cataracts
  • Genetic factors

Symptoms

  • Blurred vision
  • Poor depth perception
  • Eye wandering inward or outward
  • Difficulty reading
